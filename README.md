Harmony is a lightweight Python script that upgrades your matchmaking experience by automatically accepting queue pops, banning your specified champion, and locking in your preferred pick. Works silently in the background so you can step away while queuing.

---

## Features

- Auto-Accept: Instantly detects and accepts the matchmaking ready check.
- Auto-Pick: Insta-locks your chosen champion the moment it becomes your turn.
- Auto-Ban: Automatically bans your specified champion during the ban phase.

## Requirements

- Python 3.7+
- League of Legends Client
- lcu-driver package
